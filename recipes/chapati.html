Chapati Recipe

An image showing a chapati meal

This is one of my favourite dishes i love to cook this is because of its unique and delicious taste

Ingredients

  • Wheat Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cooking Oil
  • Hot Water

Steps

  1. Pour all wheat flour in a small basin
  2. Add 5 tablespoons sugar
  3. Pour 5 cups of hot water on the wheat flour
  4. Use your hands stir mixture until it becomes solid
  5. Place a cooking pan on a source of heat like gas cooker
  6. Add one tablespoon of cooking oil to the cooking frying pan
  7. Take a dough on your hand and place in a flat surface use a wooden roller to roll dough into flat circle shape
  8. Place the flat dough in a source of heat and wait for it to burn and add some cooking oil
  9. After chapati turns yellow with black spots remove it from frying pan and place it in a sufuria
  10. Repeat procedure fr the rest of the chapatis

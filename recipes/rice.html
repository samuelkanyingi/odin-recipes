Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Boiling water
  • cooking oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Boil water on a sufuria
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of salt
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  4. Add rice and wait for rice to dry then turn of source of heat
  5. Serve while hot
